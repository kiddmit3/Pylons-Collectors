YOU MUST CONSTUCT ADDITIONAL PYLONS!


You are given a target number at the start of each game. It is up to you to select the correct pylons that will add up equal that target number. You will save your nexus from destruction if your total equals that target. Otherwise your nexus will fall to the overmind. (Don't worry though, a new nexus will be built).

The value of each pylons is randomized for each game and hidden from you.
